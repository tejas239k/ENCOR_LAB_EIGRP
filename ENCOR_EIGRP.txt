Calculation of Metric, Throughput, Delay

Metric = Throughput + Latency

Throughput = 10,000,000/BW(Min)*65536

Delay = interface delay *1,000,000(Pico Seconds) - For 10MBPS
Delay = 10,000,000/BW(GBPS) - For 1GBPS and above
Metric = (10000000/10000*65536) + (delay(sum)/1000000*65536)

Latency = Delay(sum)/1000000*65536
